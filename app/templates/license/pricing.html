Frequently Asked Questions

What is the difference between Offline and Online mode?

Offline mode runs entirely on your computer — no internet required. You download the app and run it locally. Online mode is hosted on our servers, accessible from any device with internet.

What happens after the free trial ends?

After 10 days, you'll need to choose a paid plan to continue using the software. Your data is preserved — just activate a license to resume.

What does cloud backup include?

Your entire database is automatically backed up to our secure cloud daily. If your computer fails, we can restore your data to a new machine.

Can I upgrade from Offline to Online later?

Yes. Contact us and we'll migrate your data to the online platform. You only pay the difference in price.

What is the ₹500 yearly renewal?

The Online plan includes server hosting and maintenance. The ₹500/year renewal covers hosting costs after the first year. Offline plans have no recurring charges.
