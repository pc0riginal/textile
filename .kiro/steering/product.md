# Product Overview

Textile ERP System — a web-based business management application for textile trading companies in India.

## Core Domain
- Multi-company and multi-financial-year support
- Party management (customers, suppliers, brokers, transporters)
- Purchase challans with inventory tracking (boxes, meters)
- Inventory transfers between parties with full lineage/traceability
- Sales invoicing with GST tax calculations (CGST, SGST, IGST, TCS, TDS)
- Payment and receipt management with party ledgers
- Banking/passbook management
- Quality (fabric quality) master data
- Reporting with PDF/Excel export
- Audit logging for all key operations

## Business Context
- Indian textile trade domain — uses Indian numbering (lakhs, crores), GST compliance, and Indian state lists
- Financial year scoping — all transactional data is partitioned by company and financial year
- Inventory is tracked in boxes and meters, with transfer chains creating material lineage
- Party types: customer, supplier, both, broker, transporter
